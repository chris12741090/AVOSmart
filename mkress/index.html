Choose the Perfect Avocados

Here are some tips from your friends at Mission Avocado,to help you select the best Avocados. Whether you are eating them today or saving for that perfect summer party!!

COLOR IS NOT AN INDICATOR OF RIPENESS

Determining the correct ripeness can help you find the perfect Avocado.

Ripe Stage 1: Hard

Fresh off the tree, the avocado is very hard with no give. 5+ days until ripe.

Ripe Stage 2: Pre-conditioned>

Ripening has begun, approximately 4-5 days until ripe.

Ripe Stage 3: Breaking

Still firm but yields slightly to pressure, approximately 2 days until ripe.

Ripe Stage 4: Firm Ripe

The avocado is ripe and yields slightly to gentle pressure.

Ripe Stage 5: Ripe

The avocado is RIPE yields easily to gentle pressure.

Loaded Bacon Guacamole

  1. 4 Large ripe Mission avocados, halved, pitted, and scooped out.
  2. ½ lb, thick cut bacon, cooked crisp and cut into ½ inch pieces
  3. 1 cup cherry tomatoes, quartered.
  4. ½ tsp salt.
  5. ¼ tsp pepper.
  6. ⅓ cup basil leaves, chopped.
  7. 3 cloves garlic, minced.
  8. 2 tsp fresh squeezed lemon juice.

Cheesecake with Avocado Mousse

For the Crust:
  1. 2 cups ground gingersnap cookies.
  2. ½ cup toasted unsweetened coconut.
  3. 1 cup white sugar.
  4. 1 stick melted butter.
  5. 1 tsp cinnamon.
  6. ¼ tsp ground ginger.
  7. 1 tsp salt.
For the Filling:
  1. 4 eight-ounce packages of cream cheese.
  2. 1 ½ cups white sugar plus ¼ cup cornstarch, mixed.
  3. 1 cup full fat sour cream.
  4. 1 stick melted butter.
  5. ½ tsp salt.
  6. 1 tsp vanilla.
For the Mousse:
  1. 2 ripe Mission avocados.
  2. ½ cup dark chocolate chips.
  3. ¼ cup honey.
  4. ⅓ dark cocoa powder.
  5. 2 tsp vanilla.
  6. ½ tsp salt.
Optional Toppings:
  1. Candied ginger.
  2. Ginger snap pieces.
  3. Toasted coconut.

Meet our World Famous Avocados

California

Global Headquarters Oxnard, California

We got our start by farming and shipping avocados from California.
Today we continue to farm throughout California and have developed partnerships
with the state’s top growers to provide high-quality avocados to markets all
over the globe.


Peru

Avocados from Peru

We own 2,600 hectares of avocados grown in the central desert regions of Peru
as well as along some of the most picturesque beaches in the highlands off the northern coast.
These unique growing conditions provide us with enhanced supply to meet strong demand during the
summer months.


Mexico

Uruapan, Zamora, Nayarit & Jalisco

Mexico is thought to be the birthplace of the avocado. Today, you’ll find a vast network of
Mission Produce grower partnerships throughout the country’s prime growing regions.


Chile

With its amazing geographical setting and frost free avocado growing conditions,
Chile is renowned for producing ultra-high quality avocados. In 1986 Mission produce
was the FIRST handler to import avocados from chile to North America
